Backpacking

"The mountains are calling and I must go." - John Muir

I love backpacking! In my humble opinion it is some of the best fun you can have in life. Especially if you do it with friends! I started backpacking shortly after highscool. It took me a while to save up the money for good gear but it was totally worth it!

There is a common miscopection that backpacking is very dangerous and that it requires the most demanding of mountaineering skills. This is far from the truth. Before I started going on trips I had only completed normal hikes. While I was in good shape from doing sports in high school I was far from a mountineer. If I can start doing this than anyone can.... more or less haha.

Some of the most favorte trips that I've been on so far have been in nothern Idaho (about 1.5hrs from where I grew up). I've been on many trips (click to see list)
